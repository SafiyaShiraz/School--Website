What's new

  • New school Policy

    Because of the amount of kids getting hurt because of them, the school decided to ban climbing on ice slopes. Kids were pushing and shoving resulting in many students suffering from injuries. A few kids got concussions and the school rrefused to put up with this absurd and dangerous behavior. They came in the classes and gave speeches on why you shouldn't push people on elevated surfaces and how it was banned. Now, if you do this you will be suspended.

  • Gym Times

    Gym times have been slightly altered and made shorter as a temporary punishment towards some kids who were pushing on the ice. It's not very serious but gym time gets shortened for the entire class. It's to make sure no one climbing and pushing anymore.
